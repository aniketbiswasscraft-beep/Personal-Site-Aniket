02. CHRONOLOGY

THE STORY UNFOLDING

A timeline of risk, constant learning, and practical marketing economics.

CH 01
2018 - 2019

The Curiosity Phase

Began by breaking apart web structures, understanding search algorithms, and reverse-engineering organic traffic. I was hooked by the immediate feedback loop of digital networks. The goal was simple: figure out why people click.

SEO Arbitrage Organic Traffic Conversion Testing
CH 02
2019 - 2020

The First Wins

Took on my first consulting clients in highly competitive local spaces. Managing small budgets taught me the value of copywriting, direct response, and clear message positioning. Every campaign became a lesson in consumer psychology.

Direct Response Copywriting Client Acquisition
CH 03
2020 - 2021

Buying Attention

Co-founded and scaled private DTC e-commerce brands. Designed landing pages, directed visual creative assets, and began buying media directly. Managing my own money forced me to realize that traffic is meaningless without robust unit economics.

DTC Bootstrap Paid Acquisition Unit Economics
CH 04
2021 - 2023

Learning Scale

Led performance marketing across larger brands. Transitioned from micro-budgets to deploying large-scale ad spends. Scaled acquisition by focusing on rapid creative testing cycles to sustain ROAS as budgets scaled.

Media Buying Ad Account Scaling Creative Direction
CH 05
2023 - 2025

Building Systems

Bridged marketing with data. Developed custom reporting pipelines and attribution models to monitor spend across multiple channels. The goal was to remove guesswork and make creative deployment systematic.

Tracking Integrations Attribution Models Data Automation
CH 06
2025 & Beyond

What's Next?

Applying predictive growth modules and AI-assisted workflows to scale DTC ventures into new categories. Still curious, still testing, and focused on the next order of magnitude. This is just the runway.

AI Workflows Next-Gen DTC Long Runway
